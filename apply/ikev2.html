Experimental

The IPv6 Ready Logo Committee would like to announce that the IKEv2 Program will be moving to experimental.

The test specifications and test tools will be left on the website for vendor use, however the tools will not be supported by the IPv6 Ready Logo Committee.

Note : EAP authenticaiton functions are outside of the scope of the requirement at this version, the recommended specifications of EAP-MD5 and EAP-TLS are described at following Appendix-A and Appendix-B respectively.
